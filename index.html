Welcome!

Welcome to Solidify, the free, open-source, and interactive Blockchain, Smart Contracts, and Solidity tutorial.

Whether you have or do not have prior experience with programming or blockchain, this tutorial is intended for everyone who wishes to start their journey learning about Blockchain, Smart Contracts, and Solidity.

What makes this tutorial different?

  • No need to download anything.
  • If you can open this website, you can learn!
  • Interactive and integrated feedback.
  • You can contribute to this tutorial!

Learn the basics of Blockchain

This tutorial will introduce the followings about blockchain, including:

  • The basic concept of blockchain
  • The working mechanism of blockchain
  • Applications of blockchain

Learn the basics of Smart Contracts

This tutorial will also introduce smart contracts, including:

  • The basic concept of smart contracts
  • The working mechanism of smart contracts
  • Benefits of smart contracts

Learn the basics of Solidity

Solidity is a programming language used to implement smart contracts. In this tutorial, you will get a chance to learn the programming language yourself and implement your own smart contracts.

Test your knowledge

Throughout this tutorial, there will be quizzes and practice tests for you to get hands-on experiences and practice your understanding of the knowledge.
